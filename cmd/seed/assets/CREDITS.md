# Demo pet image credits

These images are bundled **only** as placeholder content for the local demo seed
(`cmd/seed`); they are not part of the application's licensed source. They are
embedded so the system stays fully local (the challenge forbids fetching from
externally hosted services at runtime).

- **Dogs** (`dog-*.jpg`) — https://placedog.net, a free dog placeholder service.
- **Cats** (`cat-*`) — https://cataas.com ("Cat as a Service"), free cat images.
- **Frogs** (`frog-*.jpg`) — https://loremflickr.com, Creative Commons images
  sourced from Flickr (CC BY); credit to the original Flickr photographers.

For a production deployment, replace these with images you own or have licensed.
